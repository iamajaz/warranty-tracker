Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Warranty Tracker ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

The Extension collects the following data locally on your device:

  • Product information: Product names, order IDs, purchase dates, warranty periods, product categories, and product image URLs extracted from Amazon and Flipkart order pages.
  • Page URLs: Order page and product page URLs to link back to your orders.

How Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API. No data is sent to any external server. We do not have servers that receive or store your data.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Payment or financial information
  • Browsing history beyond the specific order pages you interact with
  • Analytics or tracking data
  • Cookies

Network Requests

The Extension makes network requests only to Amazon and Flipkart product pages to extract warranty information. These requests are made from the background service worker and only occur when you click "Track Warranty" on a product. No data from these requests is sent to any third party.

Permissions Explained

  • storage: To save your tracked products locally on your device.
  • activeTab: To read product and order information from the current Amazon/Flipkart page.
  • notifications: To alert you when a warranty is about to expire.
  • alarms: To schedule periodic warranty expiry checks.
  • Host permissions (amazon.in, amazon.com, flipkart.com): To inject the "Track Warranty" button and read product information from order pages.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties. All data remains on your local device.

Data Deletion

You can delete individual tracked products from the Extension popup. Uninstalling the Extension will remove all stored data. You can also clear all data through Chrome's extension storage settings.

Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
